When "Assumed Safe" Becomes Tragic

Bahrain, October 15, 2025 — A four-and-a-half-year-old boy, Hassan Ali Al Mahari, died after being left inside a school van in the Damistan housing area. The child boarded the vehicle in the morning but fell asleep during the journey. After dropping off other children, the driver locked the van — unaware the boy was still inside.

His absence was only noticed later. When the driver returned to the vehicle, the child was found unconscious. He was pronounced dead at Bahrain Defence Force Hospital due to severe heat stress.
